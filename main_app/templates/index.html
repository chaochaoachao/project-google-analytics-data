Marketing Analytics, Big Data & Predictive Modelling

As a final project for culminating UofT's Data Analytics Bootcamp (Sept 2019 - March 2020), an interactive webpage showcasing DataStudio & Big Query visualizations, website customer segmentation analysis as well as revenue & time predictive modelling was completed. The problem to solve was to extract meaningful analysis on a large dataset, leveraging the outputs to provide microservices and begin to look at LTV analysis. The ultimate end-user intended is a marketing, agency or insights team member.

Explore the analysis of Google merchandise store data (2017) by navigation through the menu on the left. Additional information on methodology and work found within Documentation.

Methods used for this final project:

  • HTML/CSS/Bootstrap
  • Python Matplotlib
  • JavaScript D3.js
  • BigQuery
  • Machine Learning
